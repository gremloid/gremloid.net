A punk rock/ska punk band from El Paso Texas that pushes against right wing beliefs in support of more social platforms. Members include Joshua "Shua" Zermeño on vocals and guitar, Jeremy "Jerm the Werm" Silva on guitar, Erik "The Dawg" Duran on bass, Mickey "Funky Mick" Valdez on drums, Sam Zermeño on trombone, and Avery Johnson on trumpet. Together, Sam and Avery make the "Hornies for Justice." Mia Antifa frequently play shows around El Paso, Texas.
